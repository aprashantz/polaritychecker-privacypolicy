Privacy Policy

Welcome to Polarity Checker, developed by awarself. This Privacy Policy outlines how we protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Polarity Checker does not require any user authentication. When you use the app, you may input sentences for text classification. Please note that these sentences are not stored on our backend servers.

How We Use Your Information

The sentences you provide are used solely for the purpose of text classification to determine polarity. No personal information is collected, and the provided sentences are not stored.

Information Security

We take data security seriously. We do not store the sentences you input on our servers, reducing the risk of unauthorized access.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at awarself@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date of installation and use of Polarity Checker.
